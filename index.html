About Us

About IARRD (Indian Astronomy Rocket Research and Development) IARRD is an innovative space technology organization founded on May 27, 2023, by Harish Ragavendra Srinivasan, with the goal of advancing space exploration, astronomy, and rocket science in India. Our primary mission is to make space technologies more accessible, sustainable, and affordable, with a focus on real-world problem-solving through cutting-edge innovations. At IARRD, we strive to bridge the gap between groundbreaking space research and practical, cost-effective applications. By focusing on low-cost satellites, hybrid rocket propulsion systems, and AI-powered astronomical research tools, we aim to contribute significantly to India’s space capabilities and the global space community. Our Vision & Mission Vision: To make space exploration affordable, accessible, and sustainable, helping solve real-world problems through advanced space technologies. Mission: We focus on developing small satellite technologies, hybrid rocket propulsion systems, and AI-driven astronomical research tools. Our key projects include CubeSat missions, PrithiviSat (an AI-powered CubeSat for disaster mitigation and environmental monitoring), and the development of hybrid rocket engines. Our Work & Projects CubeSat Projects: Developing low-cost CubeSat satellites for educational, scientific, and societal purposes. PrithiviSat: A CubeSat focused on disaster mitigation, environmental monitoring, and data analytics using AI for real-time decision-making. Hybrid Rocket Propulsion: Researching and developing cost-effective hybrid rocket engines and reusable propulsion systems for space missions. Our Projects

Project 1

1. Project PrithiviSat: AI-Driven CubeSat for Disaster Mitigation and Environmental Monitoring PrithiviSat is an innovative CubeSat mission developed by IARRD with a focus on leveraging AI technology to address global environmental challenges. This affordable, low-cost CubeSat will integrate advanced AI-driven capabilities for real-time disaster mitigation, weather forecasting, and environmental monitoring. Key Features and Concepts: AI-Powered Disaster Mitigation: PrithiviSat will be equipped with multi-functional sensors capable of detecting environmental hazards such as hurricanes, wildfires, earthquakes, and floods. AI algorithms will process this data and predict possible disaster events, providing early warning systems for affected regions. Real-Time Data Transmission: The CubeSat will provide real-time transmission of data related to weather patterns, crop health, and environmental conditions, helping governments and organizations respond quickly to crises and manage resources efficiently. Environmental Monitoring: Equipped with sensors to monitor air quality, temperature, atmospheric composition, and soil moisture levels, PrithiviSat will offer valuable insights into climate change, pollution, and natural resource management. Low-Cost and Accessible: By utilizing miniaturized, off-the-shelf components and leveraging AI for processing data onboard, PrithiviSat offers an affordable solution to disaster management, environmental monitoring, and climate research for educational institutions, governments, and NGOs. Why is PrithiviSat Unique? AI-Driven Innovation: The integration of AI for disaster prediction and environmental analysis in a CubeSat format sets PrithiviSat apart as a groundbreaking project. Affordable and Scalable: The CubeSat’s low-cost approach enables wider access to real-time environmental monitoring and disaster forecasting, helping a broader range of organizations make informed decisions. Real-World Impact: With the potential to reduce the impact of natural disasters, PrithiviSat can save lives, improve agriculture, and support sustainability efforts globally. .

Project 2

2. Project Amaran: AI-Enabled Swarm Drones for Tactical Defense and Surveillance Amaran is IARRD's cutting-edge defense technology project, designed to revolutionize modern defense strategies through AI-enabled swarm drones. The project focuses on creating a swarm of autonomous drones capable of conducting surveillance, reconnaissance, and precision strikes in dynamic combat environments. Key Features and Concepts: Autonomous Swarm Operations: Amaran drones can operate as part of a swarm, using AI to collaborate and communicate seamlessly with each other. These drones can work autonomously to cover large areas, conduct surveillance, or carry out coordinated attacks without human intervention. AI-Driven Decision Making: Equipped with AI algorithms, the drones are capable of analyzing battlefield conditions in real-time and making decisions independently to adjust their strategy, ensuring greater tactical advantage. Precision Strikes and Surveillance: Amaran drones are equipped with advanced sensors for high-accuracy surveillance, tracking, and precision targeting, enabling real-time tactical data collection and immediate action in hostile environments. Modular Design for Versatility: The drone system's modular architecture allows for easy upgrades and adaptations, making it possible to swap sensors, weapons, and other components based on mission requirements. Why is Amaran Unique? Swarm Technology: The ability to operate as a coordinated swarm is a major advancement in modern warfare and surveillance. This is the first time such technology has been applied at scale to create a dynamic, real-time defense system. AI Autonomy: The AI-driven decision-making system allows Amaran drones to adapt on the fly, enhancing their effectiveness in real-world combat situations. Scalability and Flexibility: Amaran drones can scale to hundreds or thousands, covering more ground and performing specialized roles. Their modular design makes it easy to adapt them for different military, surveillance, or even civilian applications. First Mover Advantage: By pioneering swarm drone technology in defense, Amaran sets IARRD as a leader in cutting-edge defense innovations, ready to revolutionize tactical operations.

Project 3

3. Project NebulaScope: Pioneering Real-Time Deep Space Observation Project NebulaScope is an ambitious and innovative astronomy project that aims to develop the world's first real-time deep space observation system using small, cost-effective satellites and AI. The goal is to provide scientists and astronomers with unprecedented access to real-time data from the deepest reaches of space, enabling advancements in our understanding of the universe. Key Features and Concepts: Real-Time Observation of Deep Space: Unlike traditional telescopes that require long periods for observation and data collection, NebulaScope satellites will be designed to provide real-time imaging and data of distant celestial objects, such as nebulae, galaxies, and black holes. AI-Driven Data Processing: The project will leverage AI algorithms that can analyze complex astronomical data on-board, drastically reducing transmission time and allowing for rapid discoveries and immediate action for further research. Miniaturized Space Telescopes: By utilizing small satellites equipped with advanced imaging sensors and spectrometers, NebulaScope aims to revolutionize space observation at a fraction of the cost of traditional large-scale space telescopes. Environmental and Cosmological Studies: The data gathered will focus on areas like galaxy formation, cosmic radiation, and stellar birth processes. This real-time data collection will also help track changes in space weather and cosmic phenomena, contributing to space climate studies. Space Accessibility: By creating a low-cost, scalable system, NebulaScope will democratize space exploration, providing universities, research institutions, and space startups with the tools to conduct deep space observations. Why is NebulaScope Unique? First of Its Kind: No other company has attempted to provide real-time deep space observation through small, miniaturized satellites. This positions IARRD as the leader in this innovative approach. Democratizing Space: Most astronomical observations are conducted using large telescopes located on Earth or in space, requiring significant investments. NebulaScope aims to make space observation more accessible to smaller organizations, universities, and independent researchers. AI-Enabled: The integration of AI into the observation process for real-time data analysis and decision-making is an emerging frontier that IARRD will pioneer.
